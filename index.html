About Concordia

Concordia College Kalar Syedan Campus is a beacon of educational excellence, fostering a dynamic learning environment for its students. Known for its commitment to academic rigor and holistic development, the college offers a diverse range of programs spanning arts, sciences, and professional disciplines. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, Concordia College empowers students to excel in their chosen fields. Its vibrant campus life promotes cultural diversity and community engagement, preparing graduates to become leaders in their respective industries. Through innovative teaching methods and a focus on ethical values, Concordia College cultivates future-ready individuals equipped to meet global challenges.

Programs Offered

Concordia College Kalar Syedan offers a diverse array of academic programs tailored to meet the needs of aspiring students. Our offerings include FSC Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical, ICS, I.COM, and FA (IT). Each program is designed to provide a comprehensive education that equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in their chosen fields. At Concordia, we emphasize academic excellence, supported by modern facilities and a dedicated faculty committed to nurturing students' intellectual growth and personal development.

Eligiblity Criteria

To qualify for admission at Concordia College Kalar Syedan, applicants must meet specific eligibility criteria. Prospective students are required to have obtained a minimum of 50% marks in their Matriculation examinations. This criterion ensures that students entering our programs possess a solid academic foundation, preparing them to excel in their chosen courses of study. Concordia College values academic achievement and strives to provide an inclusive and supportive learning environment where every student has the opportunity to thrive.
